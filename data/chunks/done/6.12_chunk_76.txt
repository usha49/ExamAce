Pumping Lemma for Regular Languages 
Let L be a regular language. There is an integer n ≥  1 such that any string W  є  L with |w| ≥ n 
can be rewritten as W = xyz such that  
 y ≠ e,  
 |xy| ≤  n, and  
 xyiz є  L for each i ≥  0.
